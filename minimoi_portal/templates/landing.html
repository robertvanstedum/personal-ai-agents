What this is

A personal operating system built on top of large language models. Three domains running in production: a daily geopolitics and finance briefing, a German language coaching pipeline, and a professional coordination layer for managing work and intent across AI sessions.

Built from scratch, running on a Mac Mini at home, orchestrated by a small team of AI agents with clearly defined roles.

The platform

  • Curator — RSS + X bookmarks → Grok scoring → morning briefing via Telegram
  • Mein Deutsch — Vienna-tested language coaching: Lesen, Gespräche, Schreiben, Wörter
  • Guild — Chief of Staff model: intent register, domain health, ops runbooks

About

Built by Robert Van Stedum — TPM and solutions architect with a focus on API-driven platforms, AI integration, and systems that compound over time.

This platform is the origin. Patterns developed here move outward — not the other way around.
