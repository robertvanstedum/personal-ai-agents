What this is

A personal AI agent system built around one principle: the cloud LLMs have the world's knowledge — that problem is largely solved. The hard part is acting in your specific situation. Your history. Your goals. Your risk tolerance.

General intelligence is widely available now. Specific intelligence — the kind that knows you and acts for you — isn't. That's what this builds toward.

Local memory, model-agnostic pipeline, swappable LLMs. Context and preferences stay with you. Switch models, switch providers, go offline — the agent still knows you.

What's running

  • Curator v1.1 — Geopolitics and finance briefing. ~700 RSS and X candidates scored daily by a reasoning model with your learned profile. Top 20 to the web portal, top 10 to Telegram at 7 AM. In daily use since February 2026.
  • Mein Deutsch v0.9 beta — German language coaching pipeline. Vienna-tested. Lesen, Gespräche, Schreiben, Wörter. Anki cards earned from real friction, not passive review.
  • Guild — Professional operating system. Chief of Staff model: intent register, domain health, four-cabinet coordination layer.
