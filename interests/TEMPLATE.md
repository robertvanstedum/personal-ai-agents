## Usage Examples

### Deep Dive
```markdown
[DEEP-DIVE] China's gold accumulation strategy
- Bought 1.5M oz in Jan, 3rd month straight
- Simultaneously dumping US Treasuries
- Questions:
  - What's the target allocation?
  - Coordinated with other BRICs?
  - Timeline for dollar alternatives?
- Next: Research other central bank patterns
```

### This Week
```markdown
[THIS-WEEK] Iranian sanctions escalation
- US considering tanker seizures
- Legal basis?
- Compare to historical precedents (Iraq, Libya)
```

### Backlog
```markdown
[BACKLOG] Fed's inflation target shift discussion
- Political or economic rationale?
- Check back after March FOMC meeting
```

### Mute
```markdown
[MUTE] Sports betting / prediction markets
- Not aligned with current geopolitics focus
- Mute for next week, revisit later
```

## Tips

1. **Use checkboxes** - Mark when you've explored a topic
2. **Add dates** - Note when interest started/ended
3. **Link articles** - Reference specific briefing items
4. **Be honest** - Mute what doesn't serve you right now
5. **Review weekly** - Prune [BACKLOG], promote [THIS-WEEK] â†’ [DEEP-DIVE]
